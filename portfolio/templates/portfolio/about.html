About Network

Africa's most innovative job platform – connecting talent with opportunity across the continent.

Network was founded in Zambia with a vision to transform how professionals and employers connect in Africa. We believe that the right opportunity can change a life, and the right talent can transform a business. Our platform uses advanced matching algorithms to ensure that job seekers find positions that truly fit their skills and aspirations, while employers discover candidates who will drive their organisations forward.

With a focus on trust, transparency, and community, Network is more than a job board – it's a professional network where you can grow your career, share insights, and build meaningful connections. We are proud to serve job seekers and employers across the continent, from Lusaka to Lagos, Nairobi to Cape Town.

Our Mission

To empower Africa's workforce by creating a seamless, intelligent platform for professional growth and talent discovery.

Our Values
  • Integrity: We build trust through transparency and fairness.
  • Innovation: We constantly evolve to meet the needs of our users.
  • Community: We foster a supportive environment for professionals.
